But the single most powerful upgrade you can make is giving it something it does not have by default: your voice.Without a tone and language document, Claude will write rewrites that are clear, credible, and well-structured, but they will sound like a skilled generic writer rather than you specifically. The more you can teach it about how you actually speak and write, the more the output will feel like something you would genuinely say rather than something you need to heavily edit before using.Creating this document does not need to be complicated. It is simply a written record of the things that make your communication distinctly yours.Common phrases and expressions. Think about the words and phrases that come naturally to you when you explain what you do in conversation. The analogies you reach for. The way you open a thought or close a point. If you tend to say "the honest truth is" or "here is what I have found" or "in my experience," write those down. These small verbal signatures are what make writing feel human and specific.Topics and themes you do not address. Every strong personal brand has things it deliberately does not say. If you find motivational language hollow, note that down. If you never make promises about income or results that you cannot substantiate, write that in. If there are industry debates you consciously stay out of, or framings of your work that you find reductive, include them. Knowing what you do not say is just as important as knowing what you do.Banned words and AI phrases. There is a predictable set of words that AI tools gravitate towards that have been so overused they now signal immediately that a human did not write the content. Words like leverage, foster, delve, cultivate, game-changer, and landscape used in the abstract. Phrases like serves as, stands as, and it is worth noting. Structures like the triple denial hook, the it is not X it is Y reframe, and the if you are serious about X do this close. List every word or phrase that makes you wince when you read it, and tell Claude never to use them.Your formatting preferences. If you prefer long flowing sentences over short punchy fragments, say so. If you find bullet-heavy writing impersonal, note that down. If you have a preference for British English, specific punctuation habits, or a particular way of structuring an argument, include all of it.Once you have written this document, upload it to your project knowledge base alongside the other two files. Claude will draw on it automatically every time it writes for you, and the difference in how closely the output matches your natural voice will be immediately noticeable.How To Take it FurtherYou should have now audited and edited your profile so that it works as a conversion funnel.
